UseSHA2Fingerprint()

Syntax

UseSHA2Fingerprint()
Beschreibung
Registriert das SHA2 Fingerprint Plugin für die weitere Nutzung. Die standardmäßigen 224-Bit, 256-Bit, 384-Bit und 512-Bit Varianten werden unterstützt.

Parameter

Diese Funktion hat keine Parameter.

Rückgabewert

Diese Funktion gibt keinen Wert zurück.

Anmerkungen

Vom (englischen) Wikipedia: SHA-2 includes significant changes from its predecessor, SHA-1. In 2005, an algorithm emerged for finding SHA-1 collisions in about 2000-times fewer steps than was previously thought possible. Although (as of 2015) no example of a SHA-1 collision has been published yet, the security margin left by SHA-1 is weaker than intended, and its use is therefore no longer recommended for applications that depend on collision resistance, such as digital signatures. Although SHA-2 bears some similarity to the SHA-1 algorithm, these attacks have not been successfully extended to SHA-2.

Beispiel

  UseSHA2Fingerprint()

  Debug StringFingerprint("yourpassword", #PB_Cipher_SHA2, 512) ; verwende die SHA2-512 Variante
